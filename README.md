# Retail Sales Trend Analysis (1993–2019)
## Overview
This project analyzes U.S. retail sales over time to identify long-term trends, seasonal patterns, and the impact of major economic events.
## Objective
To understand how retail sales have changed over time and identify key patterns in consumer spending.
## Dataset
U.S. Retail Sales Time Series dataset (monthly data from 1993 to 2019)
## Tools Used
- Microsoft Excel  
- Data Cleaning  
- Data Visualization  
## Key Insights
- Retail sales show a strong upward trend over time  
- Clear seasonal spikes appear, likely due to holiday shopping  
- A noticeable decline occurs around 2008–2009 (financial crisis)  
- Sales recover and continue growing after downturns
